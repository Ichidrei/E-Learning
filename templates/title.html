How to Play

Welcome to InQUIZition! Test your knowledge and skill across different levels of challenge.

  • Easy Mode: Each scenario has 2 questions. Answer both correctly to earn 1 point per scenario and reach 5 points to move up.
  • Moderate Mode: You must complete at least 4 correct scenarios to advance.
  • Hard Mode: Each scenario has 3 questions. Clear 3 correct scenarios to prove your mastery.

Your progress and accuracy will be tracked. Aim for at least 75% accuracy to unlock the next difficulty.

  • You have 1 minute to answer each question.
  • Use the Hint button if you need help.
  • After finishing a difficulty, visit the Progress Page to review your growth.

Get ready for the InQUIZition and show how sharp your mind truly is!
